Why this post?

With the start of my PhD, I want to focus more and more on the interpretability of models, in as much detail as possible. Inspired by the various works in the literature, in particular that which has been carried out by Anthropic (e.g. here and here), Google DeepMind and a number of other researchers around the world, I would like to be able to contribute scientific output on this topic. I also hope that the material and sources here may be useful for someone else who, at the same time or in the near future, decides to explore this field with me.

What will be the first steps?
